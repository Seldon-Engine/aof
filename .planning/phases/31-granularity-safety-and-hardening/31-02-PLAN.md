<objective>
Add callback loop prevention via depth limiting and daemon restart recovery for pending deliveries.

Purpose: (1) Prevent infinite callback chains by tracking callbackDepth on task frontmatter and skipping delivery at depth >= 3. (2) Ensure pending subscriptions (never-attempted or partially-attempted) are recovered on daemon restart by expanding retryPendingDeliveries to handle deliveryAttempts === 0 cases.

Output: callbackDepth on TaskFrontmatter, depth checking in delivery functions, depth propagation in callback session spawning, expanded recovery scan in retryPendingDeliveries, and comprehensive tests.
</objective>
